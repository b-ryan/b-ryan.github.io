Pandora and Xmonad

2013-02-17

I started using xmonad a few months ago. I'm trying to do my best to never use a mouse. I'm also very into automating things. Shortly after I started using it, I noticed that whenever I needed to pause Pandora, I needed to switch to Chrome, switch to the correct tab, and pause the player. I figured there's probably a better way to do this. In comes pianobar.

Pianobar is a command-line Pandora player.
